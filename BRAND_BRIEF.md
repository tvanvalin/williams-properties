## Typography Direction (For Rebuild)
The current Palatino Linotype conveys classic/traditional elegance. For the rebuild, we need fonts that:
- Honor the historic, established character (120+ year family business)
- Feel premium and refined — not corporate
- Work beautifully at both display and body sizes

**Recommended pairing:**
- **Display/Headings:** Playfair Display — elegant serif with character, evokes historic luxury
- **Body:** DM Sans — clean, modern, highly readable — creates contrast with the classic headings

## Design Direction for Rebuild
**Aesthetic:** "Refined heritage editorial" — a design that feels like a premium architectural magazine. Warm, grounded colors inspired by the brick, wood, and craftsman details of the actual properties. Clean modern layout that lets the historic photography be the hero.

**Color Direction:**
- Primary: Deep warm charcoal (#1C1917) — grounded, sophisticated
- Secondary: Warm cream/ivory (#FAF7F2) — inviting, clean  
- Accent: Burnished copper/terracotta (#B45309) — connects to brick buildings, warmth
- Text on light: Rich dark (#292524)
- Text on dark: Warm white (#FAFAF9)

**Differentiation:** The single unforgettable thing: the marriage of historic architectural photography with modern, premium web design — making you FEEL the character of these 100+ year old buildings through the screen.
