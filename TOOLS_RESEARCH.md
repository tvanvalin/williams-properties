| Tool | Found On | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Rent Affordability Calculator | Zillow, SmartAsset, NerdWallet, Apartments.com | Input income/debts, output max affordable rent |

| 2 | Move-In Cost Calculator | HIGH | HIGH | MEDIUM | EASY |
| 3 | Rent Affordability Calculator | HIGH | HIGH | LOW | EASY |

## Final Selection (3 Tools)

### 1. "Find Your Apartment" Quiz
5-7 question interactive quiz matching users to buildings based on budget, bedrooms, pets, style preference, and must-have features. Outputs matched buildings with CTAs. Highest differentiation — almost no small property managers do this.

### 2. Move-In Cost Calculator
Inputs: building, unit type, pet type. Outputs: first month rent + deposit + pet deposit + utility estimate = total move-in cost. Builds radical transparency and trust.

### 3. Rent Affordability Calculator
Inputs: income, debts. Outputs: recommended max rent with matched Williams buildings. Natural funnel qualifier.

**Together these form a conversion funnel:** Affordability → Quiz → Move-In Cost.
